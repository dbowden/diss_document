#Data Collection on Rebel Attributes

- Broad religion - Christianity, Islam, etc.
- Religious sect - Catholic, Shiia, etc.
- Language
- Ethnicity
- Geographic spread
- Sector - students, farmers/peasants, factory workers, etc.
- Ideology primary - Islamism, Leninism, etc.
- Ideology secondary
- Ideology tertiary

- For each, a 1-5 (or 1-3) rating of centrality to identity
